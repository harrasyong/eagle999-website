Our Security Services

At EAGLE 999 SDN BHD, we offer a comprehensive range of security solutions tailored to various industries, environments, and client needs. Our goal is to deliver peace of mind through reliability, professionalism, and safety-focused practices.

Core Security Services

  • Commercial & Industrial Security
    Secure factories, offices, warehouses, and construction sites with trained personnel and patrol systems.
  • Residential & Gated Community Security
    Provide safety and control in apartments, gated neighborhoods, and private residences with access control and perimeter monitoring.
  • Event Security & Crowd Control
    Manage high-traffic events, corporate functions, festivals, and private functions with expert crowd control and emergency planning.
  • VIP Escort & Executive Protection
    Ensure personal safety for high-profile individuals through close protection, discreet escort, and secure transport protocols.

Specialized Security Services

  • Armed and Unarmed Guards
    Deploy personnel depending on threat levels, site sensitivity, and compliance with Malaysian law.
  • Mobile Patrol Units
    Provide scheduled or random patrols, ideal for larger sites, night security, or vacant property monitoring.
  • Night Guard Services
    Protect against theft, intrusion, and vandalism during off-hours with overnight on-site security.

Technology-Integrated Solutions

  • CCTV Monitoring
    Monitor your property with 24/7 surveillance supported by real-time alerts and recorded footage.
  • Access Control Systems
    Limit unauthorized entry with secure card/fingerprint access, boom gates, and visitor management systems.
  • Incident Reporting Systems
    Real-time incident reports, guard tour tracking, and digital reporting tools for transparency and documentation.
