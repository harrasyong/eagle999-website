About Us

EAGLE 999 SDN BHD is a fully licensed and certified private security company established in 2010. Based in Malaysia, we specialize in providing tailored security solutions for commercial, industrial, residential, and governmental sectors. Our team is made up of highly trained, experienced, and disciplined personnel – many with military or law enforcement backgrounds.

Our Vision

To be Malaysia’s most trusted and respected private security service provider, setting the standard for professionalism, reliability, and innovation in the industry.

Our Mission

To protect our clients' assets, properties, and people by delivering high-quality security solutions built on integrity, discipline, and operational excellence.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency in all our services.
  • Professionalism: Our team maintains the highest standards of conduct, appearance, and performance.
  • Commitment: We are dedicated to the safety and satisfaction of our clients.
  • Responsiveness: We act quickly and effectively in emergencies and critical situations.
  • Continuous Improvement: We invest in training, technology, and innovation to remain at the forefront of the security industry.

Our Team

Led by seasoned professionals with decades of experience in military, policing, and private security, our management team ensures every operation is strategically planned and professionally executed. Our security personnel are handpicked, undergo intensive training, and are regularly evaluated to maintain peak performance standards.
